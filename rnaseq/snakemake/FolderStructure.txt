- postsnakemake:
	- DESeq2: Differential Expression results
	- Heatmap: Heatmap visualization of DEG (Differentially Expressed Genes)
	- GSEA: GSEA results
- snakemake: 
	- fastqc: FastQC report for raw read files
	- bam_qc: information on mapping rate and other alignment quality control. 'samstat' is most helpful
	- bigWig: read coverage tracks to help visualize read coverage for individual genes. Can be viewed by IGV (https://software.broadinstitute.org/software/igv/) or UCSC genome browser
	- config.yaml: can find information about genome, annotation here
